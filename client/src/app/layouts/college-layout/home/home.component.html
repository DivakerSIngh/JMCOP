Overview

WELCOME TO JAGMEET COLLEGE OF PHARMACY (JMCOP)

Jagmeet College of Pharmacy, located at Plot No. 41, Knowledge Park – III, Greater Noida ,as a part of the student’s centric services of Metro Group of Hospitals founded in 1997 by, Dr.Purshotam Lal (Padma Vibhushan, Padma Bhushan and Dr. B.C. Roy National Awardee), a pioneer of Interventional Cardiology in India, Metro Group of Hospitals is a group of 13 (2,500+ beds) flourishing hospitals, varied specialties situated in Delhi, Noida, Meerut, Haryana, Haridwar, Gurgaon, Gujarat and Rajasthan.

Society of Metro Group for Education and Medical Research has initiated to established Metro College of Health Sciences & Research to impart Quality Education in area of Health Sciences, Metro College of Pharmacy is one of the wing which will offer different Pharmacy program to equip young students to develop a strong set of competencies desired in today’s cutting edge -competitive world.

The Jagmeet College of Pharmacy is established in a 2.68 acres land with all infrastructure like Class Room, Laboratories, Machine Room, Instrumentation Room, Library, Play Ground, Cafeteria, Animal House and Herbal Garden etc. The Pharmacy College is affiliated to Dr.A.P.J.Abdul Kalam Technical University Lucknow and Board of Technical Education, Lucknow and approval by Pharmacy Council of India and All India Council for Technical Education & Ministry of HRD New Delhi. It is located amidst environment friendly and lush green educational hub of Greater Noida, by the side of 8- lane Noida - Greater Noida Express way is a sprawling and serene campus at a distance of about 30 minutes drive from New Delhi Border.
