JMCOP Canteen

Cafeteria/Canteen features :-

  • A Canteen facility is also available in the campus with wide range of services.
  • Canteen provides all normal daily requirements of the students on payment basis.
  • It remains open 24 hrs on working as well as nonworking days . Nescafe, Burger, Momos etc are served in these canteen along with other items.
  • Beside the episodic events, Cafeteria, is the place where students get a lot relaxation and enjoy their free time.
